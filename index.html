About Us

Who we are...

  • Our Story

    The PAWTOPIA dream came from a lifetime of owning and loving cats & dogs. Doggy day care was the most amazing concept imaginable to us and we truly embraced it from the start. When dropping our pets off, it made us so happy to see them excited for a day playing with their human and pet friends, and the come home exhausted after an enriched day. I really wished I could be a part of this amazing, positive environment that has helped so many dogs and their owners and felt Pawtopia would be the perfect central location for myself and others. Animals welfare and happiness will always be our number one priority at PAWTOPIA.

  • WE TRUELY CARE

    We have been happily running their South Wellington kennels and cattery since 2016, providing a comfortable, safe and secure environment for your pets. They are active within the kennels, not just behind the scenes. “We love what we do, it’s all because we love hanging with the animals that we do what we do. We have always had an affinity with animals. We truly care. Each pet has its own personality, and they all appreciate being liked for who they are. We work out what makes them tick and make their stay great by working with them on that.” It is their love of animals and their welfare that makes the team at Wellington Pawtopia stand out. They really enjoy what they do. Your pet doesn’t only get looked after when you’re not able to, at Wellington Kennels & Cattery they receive boundless love and attention from the skilled and experienced team.

  • OUR GOAL

    Our goal is to provide the very best dog boarding and cattery accommodation in Wellington for your four legged friends. Leaving your mind at ease that they are in the care of highly trained staff, who are compassionate about looking after your dog or cat. With plenty of care, attention and exercise your pets will be keen to come back again and again.

DAY CARE

A day of play – Pet Daycare with a Difference...

Our friendly staff will welcome you and your beloved canine into our extended family; we strive to cater for all individual needs (and wants). Our indoor facilities ensure the dogs and cats stay as clean, dry and comfortable as possible. We have a range of furniture for the dogs to play and sleep on, and toys such as milk bottles, tuggies and dog toys for the dogs to play with. We let the dogs choose if they want to rest or play, the centre is their place. Other entertainment ranges from playing fetch through to dunking for treats in the paddling pool (in Summer).

SERVICE PRICE DESCRIPTION
Dog daycare $25/day Our indoor facilities ensure the dogs stay as clean, dry and comfortable as possible. We have a range of furniture for the dogs to play and sleep on, and toys such as milk bottles, tuggies and dog toys for the dogs to play with.
Cat daycare $20/day Our indoor facilities ensure the dogs stay as clean, dry and comfortable as possible. We have a range of play items for the cats to play around and sleep on, and toys such as milk bottles, tuggies and dog toys for the cats to play with.

GROMING

Our friendly, caring team provide expert pet grooming for your beloved pooch...

Grooming for your dog is a very personal experience that should be rewarding not just for you as an owner, but especially for your dog. We work with you to ensure that your lifestyle and requirements as well as your dog's needs are taken into consideration when discussing your dog's grooming. Our desire is to create the style and groom of your choice, while also aiming to groom your four-footed friend to the highest level for both looks and comfort. Let one of our experienced groomers take care of your dog's coat and leave it shining, healthy and more comfortable to walk and run in. You can leave your dog with us or let us visit them in the comfort of their own home. We have both the salon and home visit options available

SERVICE PRICE DESCRIPTION
Dog Groming $10/day A good brush and removal of loose fur. Tidy up of the fur, including clipping (both summer and winter options available), preformed no more than once a week, but can still be performed for a day visit. For price, look above.
Cat Groming $8/day A good brushing and removing of loose fur. Fur tidy up via clipping, preformed no more than once a week, but can still be performed for a day visit. For a price, look above.

TRAINING

Different pet training options are available for you and your pet that are both challenging and enjoyable...

We recognise that each dog and family is unique and even though some behaviour actions are common amongst dogs, it is the individuality of the environment that the dog is living in that makes any issue personal We offer honest, caring and practical solutions that you will find easy to implement. We look to cater for any changes that may be required to cause as minimal disruption to your lifestyle. For dog-to-dog behaviour issues we recommend a one-on-one personal consultation to assess the level of the issue. Once an assessment is made, a recommendation may be made to complete a class such as the Good manners and focus class or more individual training.

SERVICE PRICE DESCRIPTION
Dog training $150 full Traning Basic training includes: sit, lay down, stay, come, as well as toilet training.
Cat training $70 full Traning Simple things, like teaching them to not scratch up furniture, to use a litter box, and more.
