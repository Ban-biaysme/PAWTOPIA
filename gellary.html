WE TRUELY CARE!!

When you are away from your beloved pet it helps to know that they are being cared for in a safe, healthy and entertaining environment. PAWTOPIA Pet Hotel & pet Daycare in Wellington offers you the very best in short or long term pet care. Our pet daycare service is perfect for those dogs and cats who need some company and special attention while their human is at work.

All animals are kept indoors in spacious purpose built units in a temperature controlled room. Your pets will be given top quality food, lots of love and they'll have every opportunity to play, relax and be happy.
